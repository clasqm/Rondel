Markdown Cheat sheet for Rondel 
=====================

Rondel (or rather the mkd2html utlity) actually supports **all** canonical markdown structures plus a few extensions, but not all alternatives will be presented in Rondel's menu system.

Paragraphs are marked by an empty line. A newline can be forced by two spaces at the end of a previous line.

#### <a name="Lists">Lists
##### Code:
    * Red
    * Green
    * Blue
##### Results:
* Red
* Green
* Blue

While we're at it,let's demonstrate the use of regular HTML tags:
##### Code:
    * <font color="red">Red</font>
    * <font color="green">Green</font>
    * <font color="blue">Blue</font>

#####Results:
* <font color="red">Red</font>
* <font color="green">Green</font>
* <font color="blue">Blue</font>

####An ordered list
##### Code:
    1. Bird
    3. McHale
    2. Parish

#####Results:
1. Bird
3. McHale
2. Parish

Note that a list should be preceded by either a heading or a blank line. Extra blank lines will be ignored by the parser.

#### <a name="Document headers">Document headers

If the first three lines in the document begin with a % character, they are taken to be a document header in the form of:

 % Document title  
 % Document author  
 % Document date
